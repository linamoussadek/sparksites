How It Works

1. Target Any Geographical Area Globally

• Simply enter a location (e.g., Byward Market, Ottawa, ON; Manhattan, NY) into the search bar on our platform.

• Our advanced algorithm searches the web to identify all the small businesses in the specified area missing a website, such as coffee shops, restaurants, and nail salons.

• You'll get a detailed list of businesses that have some online presence, but are missing a crucial selling element—a website!

2. Empower Your Business with AI-Generated Websites

• For each identified business, our powerful AI crafts a customized demo website and automatically hosts it online.

• Utilizing the business's logo, incorporating its dominant colors, and generating unique textual content, each website is designed to perfectly cater to the specific needs of the business.

• These demo websites include a direct purchase button for business owners, making it easier for the business owner to purchase the website from you.

3. Sales and Customization Process

• The demo websites are designed to be a tangible showcase for business owners, demonstrating a ready-to-use website solution that eliminates the need for hiring web developers, designers, or using DIY website builders like Wix or WordPress.

• Should any website require adjustments or enhancements, our dedicated team of engineers is there to implement all necessary modifications to meet your or the owner's specifications, anytime.

What our AI generated website includes

Contact section

The phone number, email address and location found by our AI are automatically added as part of the contact section

Map widget

A Google Map widget is added as part of the contact information with a pin at the business' location

Color palette

Our AI accurately identifies and applies a business's dominant colors to the website's theme and can reverse-engineer logos with 96% accuracy—unprecedented technology.

Delivery buttons

If the business is a restaurant or coffee shop that offers delivery, our powerful AI will identify their delivery options and display them as buttons on the homepage. This feature serves as a compelling argument for business owners to enhance their sales.

Google reviews

Our advanced algorithm will fill in a review section for each business with real 4+ stars reviews posted online.

Media selection

The AI generated websites are outfitted with a Gallery section. Our algorithm filters the images found online to only use the most relevant ones to the business.
